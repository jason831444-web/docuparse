Laboratory Access Policy Implementation Memo

Purpose: This memo explains the updated process for granting after-hours laboratory access to research assistants, visiting students, and temporary project staff.

Background: The prior approval process relied on email threads and inconsistent spreadsheet notes, which made it difficult to confirm training status during safety audits.

Scope: The process applies to wet labs, secure computing rooms, shared equipment rooms, and storage spaces that require card access outside normal building hours.

Required Prerequisites: Supervisors must verify safety training, project assignment, emergency contact information, and expiration date before submitting a request.

Approval Workflow: Department coordinators review the request packet, facilities staff activate card permissions, and the research office performs a weekly exception review.

Documentation: Each request must include the student name, campus ID, sponsor, room list, access window, training certificate date, and justification for after-hours access.

Deadlines: Requests needed for Monday activation must be submitted by Thursday at 3:00 PM. Emergency requests require director approval and must be reconciled within two business days.

Audit Procedure: Coordinators must export the active-access report on the first business day of each month and compare it with project rosters and departure notices.

Revocation: Access must be removed within one business day when a project ends, a training certificate expires, or a supervisor reports a role change.

Follow-up: Send questions and exception requests to research-operations@example.edu with the subject line Lab Access Exception.
